About Us

NeuroTechX is a non-profit organization whose mission is to build a strong global neurotechnology community by providing key resources and learning opportunities, and by being leaders in local and world-wide technological initiatives.

We bring enthusiasts, hackers, and experts together to drive innovation and foster collaboration at local and international scales. We envision an inclusive and collaborative ecosystem of researchers, experts, hackers and enthusiasts and accessible resources and learning opportunities for all.

Community

To become a practical reality, brain technology needs to be supported by a diverse and dynamic community of experts, entrepreneurs and enthusiasts. NeuroTechX is a connector of this community and a facilitator of important and inclusive conversations about the future of neurotechnology.


Education

NeuroTechX firmly believes that the brain conversation must include absolutely everyone. That is why we offer accessible resources and learning opportunities for beginners or experts and everyone in between. Together, we can inspire and create neurotechnology that benefits all of society.


Innovation

NeuroTechX fosters a culture of innovation through its many initiatives and by connecting talent through its events and global network. We hope to inspire our community members to address today's challenges and opportunities with creativity and confidence.





Where We Are

NeuroTechX has chapters in over 11 cities in North America, Europe and Latin America.

Meet other neurotech enthusiasts in your local community, learn and exchange knowledge through our meetups, build innovative and collaborative projects at our Hack Nights and have access to the latest technologies at our events. If your city isn't included on our map, contact us to help bring NeuroTechX to your community!
