International Committee

The International Committee's mission is to oversee NeuroTechX Chapter Growth. From new chapters to the exsiting ones, this committee will define the guidelines, processes and expectations by working hand in hand with NeuroTechX chapters.


Scope

  • Help & Support existing Chapters.
  • Help & Support the creation of new Chapters.
  • Help to set priorities for NeuroTechX in support for its Chapters.
  • Raise Issues experienced at local levels and create plan of action to improve it.
  • Improve existing policies & processes to benefit NeuroTechX Chapters.
  • Create new policies & processes to benefit NeuroTechX Chapters.
  • Work with the Executive Director for the Legal aspect.
  • Work with the Finance Director for the Financial aspect.
  • Work with the Fundraising Director for the fundraising strategy & processes.

Roles & Members

The committee should be a mix of chapter admins, active community members & external skills.
The committee should not count more than 1 admin for a specific chapter.
The committee should not exceed 9 people.
The committee members should work closely together and as a team.
Members should bring different skills to the table.


  • Chair
    • Responsible for building the team.
    • Responsible of organizing meetings.
    • Following up with gurus.
  • Administrative Guru
    • Writing documents.
    • Taking minutes.
  • Marketing Guru
    • Helping chapters with social media.
    • Helping chapters with marketing their events.
    • Work with NTX Marketing Director for Newsletters and Campaigns.
    • Help to manage and give access to NTX Marketing Assets.
  • Scientific Event Guru
    • Guidelines & Processes for scientific events.
    • Helping chapters with their scientific events.
    • Managing access to NTX resources. (PPT, EDU, etc.)
  • Technical Event Guru
    • Guidelines & Processes for technical events.
    • Helping chapters with their technical events.
    • Managing access to NTX resources. (PPT, EDU, etc.)
  • Sponsorship & Partnership Guru
    • Guidelines & Processes for Sponsorship & Money flow.
    • Helping chapters to get sponsors & partners.
    • Work hand in hand with the Finance Director.
  • Web Guru (Wordpress)
    • NTX Main Website.
    • NTX Chapters' Websites

Expectations

    • The roles & tasks are and should be designed to average 3h to 5h of work per week.
    • It does require time to build an International Community advancing NeuroTechnology.
    • Not being committed does not only impact yourself, but all other members.
    • Failing to meet the expectations and meetings will result in a replacement.
    • Work will be remote and meetings are held through (video) calls.
    • Ideal commitment length of 1 year (2017 International Committee).


    This is a unique opportunity to work in a stimulating environment in the field of neuroscience and emerging technologies. By being part of this amazing organization, you will also be working with an international team scattered across ~20 cities of the world, including San Francisco, Montreal, New York, London, Munich, Lima, Tel Aviv, Boston, Los Angeles, and more. Help us shape the "neuro-future".

    *Please note this position is a volunteer position.

About NeurotechX

NeuroTechX brings hackers, enthusiasts, researchers and experts together to drive innovation and foster collaboration at local and international scales. Our core mission is to build a strong global neurotech community by providing key resources, learning opportunities, and by being leaders in local and worldwide technological initiatives. We believe neurotechnology is key to better understanding and to improving who we are. Join us to take part in the conversation and help shape the future of neurotechnology!
