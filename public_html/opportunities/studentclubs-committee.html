Student Club Committee

The Student Club Committee should oversee NeuroTechX Student Club Initiative.


Scope

  • Help & Support existing Student Clubs.
  • Help & Support the creation of new Student Clubs.
  • Organize the 2017 Edition / Competition(s) *
  • Explore and Secure new partnerships.
  • Should aim to grow the initiative at a large international scale.
  • Work closely with EDU to share & centralize resources & information.

Roles & Members

The committee should be a mix of undergrads, grads and members from the 2016 editions.
The committee should not count more than 1 admin from a specific student club.
The committee should not exceed 9 people.


  • Chair
    • Responsible for building the team. (voting / scouting / contacting …)
    • Responsible of organizing meetings.
    • Following up with gurus.
  • Administrative Guru
    • Responsible for writing documents.
    • Responsible for taking minutes.
  • Marketing Guru
    • Responsible for the overall marketing, outreach & visibility of the SC initiative.
    • Responsible for helping student clubs visibility.
    • Work with NTX Marketing Director for Newsletters and Campaigns.
    • Help to manage and give access to NTX Marketing Assets.
  • Education Content Guru
    • Responsible for helping SC with online materials (tutorials, projects, code, etc.)
    • Work with EDU to share and centralize resources.
  • Sponsorship Guru
    • Responsible for the Guidelines & Processes for Sponsorship & Money flow.
    • Responsible for having a cash neutral (or positive) - awesome - competition(s).
    • Work hand in hand with the Finance Director.
    • Responsible to explore potential partnerships with the industry.

Expectations

    • The roles & tasks should be designed to average 3h to 5h of work / week.
    • Possibilities with this initiative are endless. But requires a bit of work!
    • Not being committed not only impact yourself, but all other members.
    • Failing to meet the expectations and meetings will result in a replacement.
    • Work will be remote and meetings are held through (video) calls.
    • Ideal commitment length of 1 year (2017 Student Club Committee).


    This is a unique opportunity to work in a stimulating environment in the field of neuroscience and emerging technologies. By being part of this amazing organization, you will also be working with an international team scattered across ~20 cities of the world, including San Francisco, Montreal, New York, London, Munich, Lima, Tel Aviv, Boston, Los Angeles, and more.

    *Please note this position is a volunteer position.

About NeurotechX

NeuroTechX brings hackers, enthusiasts, researchers and experts together to drive innovation and foster collaboration at local and international scales. Our core mission is to build a strong global neurotech community by providing key resources, learning opportunities, and by being leaders in local and worldwide technological initiatives. We believe neurotechnology is key to better understanding and to improving who we are. Join us to take part in the conversation and help shape the future of neurotechnology!
